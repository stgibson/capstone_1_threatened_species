About Page

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to allow people who are passionate about saving threatened species to find other people who share similar interests, more specifically in which species they are interested in protecting or saving. This way, they can get into contact with each other and organize an event or organization to help the species they have a common interest in.

Instructions/Terms and Conditions

If this is your first time visiting this website, you must first create an account. Click here to navigate to the signup page. You must fill in all of the input boxes and check the checkbox to create an account. If you have already created an account, click here to login to your account. You must use the same username and password you used to create your account.

When you successfully signup/login, you will be navigated to the home page of the website. On the left-hand side of the page, you will see a searchbox. You can use this to search for any species by its full species name. We use the IUCN Red List API to retrieve our data. Neither we nor IUCN is responsible for the accuracy of the data displayed on this website. Please use this website at your own discretion, and fact check the data that you find.

When you search for a species, if the species exists and information on its threatened level exists in the IUCN Red List API, it will be displayed on the webpage, along with its threatened level. If you are interested in forming an organization or event to help this species, you may add it to your list of favorite species. This list will always be displayed on the right-hand side of the home page when you are logged in. You may also choose to remove any species on your list.

When ten users in the same city have the same species in their lists, they will all be notified via email of the usernames and email address of the other users. This allows for users to get into contact with them via email to organize an event and help the species you all have a shared interest in. By checking the checkbox on the signup page to sign up, you are agreeing to allow emails to be sent to your provided email address, and you are also agreeing to allow emails to be sent to other users with information on your username and email address.

We are not responsible for any consequences of any other users being made aware of your username, email, or approximate location. Use this website at your own risk.
